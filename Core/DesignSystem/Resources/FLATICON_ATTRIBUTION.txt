Sticker artwork
===============

The sticker pack bundled in `Stickers.xcassets/` consists of artwork
sourced from Flaticon (https://www.flaticon.com) under their Free
license, which requires attribution to the original authors and to
Flaticon itself.

Per Flaticon's Free License terms, attribution must be placed in the
credits/acknowledgements section of the application. Add the
specific author credits to Settings → About before public release.

Generic placeholder used while individual author credits are
finalised:

    Sticker icons by various authors at Flaticon (www.flaticon.com)
    used under Flaticon's Free License with attribution.

Flaticon Free License terms:
https://www.flaticon.com/license/license.pdf

If author-by-author credits aren't acceptable for the App Store
release, switch to Flaticon Premium ($12.99/month) which removes
the attribution requirement, or replace this pack with an
MIT/Apache-licensed alternative (Microsoft Fluent Emoji,
Google Noto Emoji, etc.).
